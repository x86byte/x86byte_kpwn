OrcaCyberWeapons Community

The OrcaCyberWeapons community is a focused space for reverse engineers, exploit developers, and system-level hackers. We specialize in Windows and iOS vulnerability research, low-level debugging, and modern exploitation techniques.

Partnered Communities

  • Vulnerability Researchers (Group) - A global research-oriented Telegram group originally based in the Russian security scene. It's home to vulnerability hunters and system-level researchers discussing low-level exploitation, CVEs, and reverse engineering. [Join Group]
  • Orca Cyber Weapons (Channel) - A high-quality feed of real-world exploits, vulnerability discoveries, advanced CVEs, and system-level research. Ideal for professionals tracking offensive security and low-level attack surfaces. [View Channel]

If you're passionate about security research, reverse engineering, and low-level systems, you'll find valuable content and like-minded individuals in these spaces. Everyone with genuine interest is welcome to join and learn.
